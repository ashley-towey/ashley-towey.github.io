WOOP is the early warning system for cyclists, designed to inform riders of what is going on behind them while still focussing on the road ahead. The rear light fitting includes an integrated radar sensor to detect cars approaching from behind. This information is then transmitted to your head unit or phone where lights display green, amber or red depending on how close the following vehicle is to you. The radar system offers much needed reassurance to be able to enjoy any ride, whether you’re commuting or just out for a weekend spin.



Functionality

WOOP works using ant+ and bluetooth connectivity to connect to your head unit or phone. Ant+ is used to ensure connection across different devices, meaning that WOOP will be compatible with all head units on the market. Bluetooth technology gives the rider the ability to use the WOOP sensor on their phone in a pinch.

Wahoo x WOOP

In collaboration with Wahoo, WOOP will integrate seamlessly with the light bar on the Wahoo ELEMNT range. Displaying different colours depending on how close you are being followed by a vehicle. This offers the rider instant knowledge of what’s going on behind them, while remaining focussed on the road ahead.
