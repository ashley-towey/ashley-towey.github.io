Stories for the Road

Featured in this database is a collection of responses from cyclists leaving geo-located messages for their fellow riders. The project serves as a provocation for the possible ways in which we could use technology to foster community within cycling by taking back ownership of the roads. Explore the directory, read other messages, and then contribute your own recommendation, encouragement, and story for the road.
