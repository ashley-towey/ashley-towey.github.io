Project Description

Leanne MA, UKCP, MBACP is an accredited existential psychotherapist practicing in Oxford and Henley-on-Thames. Our aim with the design approach was to provide a nurturing environment to facilitate growth, through a welcoming, caring and empathic brand voice.
