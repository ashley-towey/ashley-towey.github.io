Project Description

As part of their website re-design, Jupiter hired me to create a collection of 3D images of their underfloor heating constructions. The project included designing over 80 different construction images and a video animation to highlight the configurable nature of the Jupiter underfloor heating system. Using Blender I refreshed the look of pre-existing imagery and created the new constructions with the same design style.
